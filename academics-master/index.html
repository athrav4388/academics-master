MISSION

At Shri Radhakrishna Shaikshanic ,Sansodhan and Sevabhavi Sanstha ,Gadhinglaj,our mission is rooted in a deep commitment to providing a nurturing and inclusive educational haven for all, transcending barriers of caste, religion, gender, or region. We passionately foster a culture of scientific curiosity, ethical values, and a profound sense of national pride.
Our heartfelt dedication lies in creating a welcoming and hesitation-free learning environment, ensuring that quality education is not a privilege but a right, accessible to all. We cherish and cultivate the unique potential of every student, empowering them with global competence while encouraging enthusiastic participation in enriching co-curricular activities. Beyond academics, we embrace the noble responsibility of instilling essential human values and a sense of patriotism, shaping compassionate and responsible individuals ready to embrace the world with empathy and excellence.

ABOUT SANSTHA

Shri Radhakrishna Shaikshanic ,Sansodhan and Sevabhavi Sanstha ,Gadhinglaj. The Sanstha was established in the year 2014 at Gadhinglaj Dist .Kolhapur .Maharashtra (India). “Born to Quality Education” is the moto of the sanstha .The Gadhinglaj thasil is located in hilly area on the foots of Western Ghat of India. The students from remote area are unable to join the school and moreover they are not getting quality education. The sanstha aims to bring such students to the main stream of education and impart quality education with minimum fees or no fees
