CURRICULUM

Maharashtra State Board of Secondary & Higher Secondary Education, Pune 411004 is an Autonomous Body established under the provisions of the Maharashtra Act No. 41 of 1965. The Maharashtra State Board of Secondary & Higher Secondary Education, conducts the HSC and SSC Examinations in the state of Maharashtra through its nine Divisional Boards located at Pune, Mumbai, Aurangabad, Nasik, Kolhapur, Amravati, Latur, Nagpur and Ratnagiri. The Board conducts examination twice a year and the number of students appearing for the main examination is around 14 Lacs for HSC and 17 Lac’s SSC, for the supplementary examination around 6 Lacs students are expected HSC and SSC together. There are about 21000 schools (SSC) and 7000 (HSC) Higher Sec. Schools / Jr. colleges in the entire state.

SCHOLARSHIPS

Scholarships at Shri Radhakrishna Education Society cater to diverse backgrounds, including Central Government Scholarships for SC, ST, OBC, Minority Students, and support for children of Army officers and soldiers. Our institution ensures access to financial aid, empowering deserving students to pursue their academic dreams without constraints.

ADOPTIONS

Philanthropies have the power to transform lives at Shri Radhakrishna Education Society. By adopting financially challenged school children, their support can cover fees and expenses, enabling up to five children per year to pursue their education journey. This act of kindness unlocks doors, granting access to education for those in need, fostering a brighter future for our society.
