About School


Welcome to Shri Radhakrishna Public School: A Beacon of Educational Excellence

Established in 2016, Shri Radhakrishna Public School stands as a testament to a five-year journey dedicated to shaping young minds and nurturing future leaders. With the esteemed approval of the Government of Maharashtra, our institution offers a comprehensive educational experience, catering to students from Nursery to 10th classes, following the esteemed State Board Curriculum. At our school, our mission is deeply rooted in the belief that education is the cornerstone of a brighter future. We embrace a philosophy that echoes the sentiments of "Dnayn Vidnyan and Vikas," where knowledge, scientific temper, and holistic development lead to unparalleled growth. Our academic principles prioritize inclusivity, fostering curiosity, ethical values, and character development, shaping well-rounded individuals ready to navigate a globalized world. Here, success isn't just measured in grades but in resilience, ethical integrity, and embracing diversity. Our dedicated faculty, with over five years of experience, ensures a nurturing environment, guiding students towards academic excellence while nurturing essential human values and a sense of patriotism. Shri Radhakrishna Public School is more than an institution; it's a vibrant community fostering a culture of innovation, curiosity, and empathy. We believe in empowering our students not just with knowledge but with the skills and mindset to thrive in an ever-evolving world. Join us on this remarkable educational journey where excellence meets compassion, and every student's potential is nurtured to illuminate the path to success.

Our Philosphy

Embracing inclusivity, fostering curiosity, and nurturing values, guiding students towards holistic excellence and global citizenship.

Academics Principle

Excellence, equity, scientific curiosity, and character development, crafting well-rounded individuals for a globalized world.

Key of Success

Success hinges on resilience, knowledge thirst, ethics, diversity embracement, and a global outlook for thriving in today's world.
